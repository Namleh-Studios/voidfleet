Void Fleet — Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2-13-2026

Last Updated: 2-13-2026

Namleh Studios ("we," "us," or "our") develops and publishes Void Fleet ("the Game"), a mobile game available on iOS and Android. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

By downloading, installing, or using the Game, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Game.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Age Bracket: When you first open the Game, we ask for your birth month and year to determine your age bracket (Under 13, Teen, or Adult). We do not store your birth date — only the resulting age bracket is saved on your device.
  • Consent Preferences: Your choices regarding analytics, advertising, and personalization consent.
  • Support Correspondence: If you contact us for support, we may collect the information you provide in your communication (e.g., email address, device info, description of the issue).

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Gameplay Data: Game progress, scores, currency balances, runs completed, battles won, achievements, and similar gameplay statistics. This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.
  • Purchase Data: Records of in-app purchases (product IDs, amounts, timestamps) are stored locally on your device. Purchase transactions are processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play), subject to their respective privacy policies.
  • Analytics Events: If you consent to analytics, we may collect events such as run starts/ends, ad views, and challenge completions. These events are used to improve the Game experience.
  • Device Information: When ads are shown, our advertising partners may collect device identifiers (such as IDFA on iOS or Advertising ID on Android), device model, operating system version, IP address, and general location (country/region).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide the Game: To deliver gameplay features, save your progress, and process purchases.
  • Improve the Game: To understand how the Game is played and identify areas for improvement (with your analytics consent).
  • Serve Advertisements: To display ads within the Game (with your consent). Advertising helps keep the Game free to play.
  • Comply with Legal Obligations: To comply with applicable laws, including age-based restrictions.
  • Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries and resolve issues.

3. Legal Basis for Processing (EEA/UK Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area or United Kingdom, we process your information based on the following legal grounds:

  • Consent: For analytics, advertising, and personalization (you may withdraw consent at any time via the in-game Settings menu).
  • Contractual Necessity: To provide the Game and process purchases you initiate.
  • Legitimate Interest: For basic Game functionality, fraud prevention, and security.
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with applicable laws (e.g., age verification, COPPA).

4. Advertising and Tracking

The Game may display rewarded video advertisements. When you consent to ads:

  • Our ad mediation partner (Unity LevelPlay) and its network partners may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve and measure ads.
  • You may see personalized ads based on your interests, or contextual ads that are not personalized — depending on your consent choices and device settings.

4.1 iOS App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

On iOS, we will request your permission to track your activity across other companies' apps and websites via Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework. You can change this permission at any time in your device's Settings > Privacy > Tracking.

4.2 Opting Out of Personalized Ads

  • In-Game: Adjust your consent preferences in the Settings menu.
  • iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking.
  • Android: Settings > Privacy > Ads > Delete advertising ID.

5. Children's Privacy

We take children's privacy seriously and comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar laws worldwide.

5.1 Age Gate

The Game includes a neutral age gate that asks for birth month and year at first launch. We do not store the birth date itself — only the resulting age bracket.

5.2 Users Under 13

For users identified as under 13:

  • No advertising is displayed.
  • No in-app purchases are available.
  • No analytics data is collected.
  • No personalization is applied.
  • No social features are accessible.
  • Play-time reminders are shown every 60 minutes.
  • Child-directed treatment flags are set on all applicable SDKs.

5.3 Parental Rights

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at euzauc@gmail.com. We will promptly delete any such information.

6. Your Data Protection Rights

6.1 GDPR Rights (EEA/UK Residents)

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectify inaccurate personal data.
  • Erase your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Restrict processing of your personal data.
  • Data Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Withdraw Consent at any time via the in-game Settings menu.
  • Lodge a Complaint with your local data protection authority.

6.2 CCPA/CPRA Rights (California Residents)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act and the California Privacy Rights Act, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect, use, and share.
  • Delete your personal information.
  • Opt Out of Sale or Sharing of your personal information.
  • Non-Discrimination — we will not treat you differently for exercising your rights.

To opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal information, use the "Do Not Sell My Personal Information" toggle in the Game's Settings menu. You may also contact us at euzauc@gmail.com.

We do not knowingly sell or share the personal information of users under 16.

6.3 Exercising Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, you may:

  • Use the Settings menu in the Game to adjust consent preferences or delete your account.
  • Email us at euzauc@gmail.com.

7. Data Storage and Retention

  • Local Storage: All gameplay data, progress, preferences, and purchase records are stored locally on your device using standard platform storage (PlayerPrefs). We do not operate servers that store your personal gameplay data.
  • Third-Party Storage: Advertising partners and platform providers (Apple/Google) may store data on their servers according to their own retention policies.
  • Retention Period: Local data persists until you delete your account in-game or uninstall the Game. Since all data is stored on-device, uninstalling the Game removes all locally stored data.

8. Account Deletion

You may delete all of your data at any time using the "Delete Account" option in the Game's Settings menu. This permanently erases all locally stored data, including:

  • Game progress, achievements, and statistics
  • Purchase records and virtual currency balances
  • Battle pass progress
  • Daily challenge progress and login streaks
  • All consent and privacy preferences

After deletion, the Game will restart with the initial age gate and consent flow. Note that purchase transactions processed by Apple or Google are subject to their respective data retention policies.

9. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. Since gameplay data is stored locally on your device, it is protected by your device's built-in security features (passcode, biometric lock, encryption). Third-party SDK communications use industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS).

10. International Data Transfers

If you use the Game outside the United States, your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States or other countries where our third-party partners operate. These transfers are necessary to serve advertisements and process purchases. Where required, appropriate safeguards (such as Standard Contractual Clauses) are in place.

11. Do Not Track

The Game responds to iOS App Tracking Transparency preferences and in-game consent settings. There is no universal "Do Not Track" standard for mobile applications, but we honor your opt-out choices as described in Section 4.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. If changes are material, we will notify you through the Game (e.g., by re-displaying the consent dialog). Your continued use of the Game after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
